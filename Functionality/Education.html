Education

Education application of ChatGPT

Education function


Education function of the ChatGPT is mainly reflected in academic part, includes:

  • Teach users how to solve tricky questions, such as math or physics questions.
  • Teach users how to improve their language skills with the instruction
  • Stimulate users productivity and creativity

Educational benefits


Students can be more creative when doing their homework with educational application.
Since ChatGPT in this model will give some instructions instead of outputting answers directly.
it's more specific in the educational field to prevent students from directly copying without thinking.
And also, using its basic functions, users can check their own work whether it is correct or not. In this case, students can understand where they made mistakes as soon as possible.

If students encounter difficulties during the school math lessons, they can ask Math to tell them how to solve it.
For example, if users wonder how to calculate the length of a curve through calculus, they can type as shown on the left:
Math explains it in four periods, which are conception, formula, step-by-step and code.
The most interesting part is the code. If users want to do the calculation in computer languages such as python, they can copy the codes on the button to the compiler so that users can calculate the formula faster.
