Writing function


Writing function of the ChatGPT is mainly reflected in academic part, includes:

  • Format recommendations
  • Use of humanizing content to pass the AI detection
  • Text generation within some format like essays, novels, poems etc.


Writing benefits


Using this part, people can produce their literature work more efficiently in some format since it can be used for brain storming , which may generate some unexpected ideas for users.

Potential impact on the industry


The significant impact may be reflected on the academic performance of students, who may use ChatGPT to help them write academic essays.

  • Even though there are some AI detectors to check students' work, they still can use AI like Humanizer Pro to rewrite the generative part and make it is it looks like written by human, which may do harm to their creativity and their own study.
  • Also, the detection of AI may increase the burden on teachers who check the homework by themselves.
  • Further more, people will improve their productivity with its help.
  • However, it will reduce the innovation and creativity of talents if they rely too much on ChatGPT without their own thoughts.
